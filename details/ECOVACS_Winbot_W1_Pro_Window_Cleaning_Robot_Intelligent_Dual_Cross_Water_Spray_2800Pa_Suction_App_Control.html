ECOVACS Winbot W1 Pro Window Cleaning Robot

Say goodbye to the hassle of window cleaning with the ECOVACS Winbot W1 Pro. Designed to make the process easy, safe, and efficient, this intelligent robot is a must-have for modern homes.

Smart Cleaning at Your Fingertips

Featuring intelligent dual cross water spray technology, the Winbot W1 Pro delivers even moisture across the glass. This ensures that even the toughest stains and dirt are effortlessly removed.

With 2800Pa suction, it maintains a strong grip on the window surface, eliminating the need for ladder use or manual support. All while offering clean results that are both deep and spotless.

Designed for Precision and Safety

The advanced edge detection technology ensures the robot navigates safely around the edges of frameless windows. Combined with a stable movement system and high-quality materials, it guarantees durable and reliable performance.

Thanks to the WIN SLAM 3.0 path planning, the robot can operate in three modes: quick, deep, and spot stain removal. This flexibility allows you to tailor the cleaning experience to your needs.

Convenient and Easy to Use

Control the Winbot W1 Pro directly from your smartphone with the intuitive app. This lets you start or stop the cleaning process from anywhere, making it an ideal addition to your smart home ecosystem.

Whether you're cleaning large glass panels or small windows, the robot adjusts seamlessly to different sizes. It keeps your home looking sparkling clean, without the physical strain.

The Perfect Solution for Busy Homes

Life is busy, and you deserve a cleaner that works as hard as you do. The ECOVACS Winbot W1 Pro takes the stress out of window maintenance, allowing you to focus on what matters.

From day-to-day dust to stubborn grime, this robot handles it all with ease. It’s an investment in efficiency and quality, designed to make your life better.
