Roborock Q10 S5+ – Your Smart Cleaning Partner

Experience advanced cleaning technology that adapts to your lifestyle and keeps your home spotless with minimal effort.

The Roborock Q10 S5+ comes with a large 2.7L dust bag, allowing for extended cleaning sessions without the need to stop and empty it frequently. Perfect for busy families and pet owners who want peace of mind and convenience.

10,000 Pa HyperForce Suction

With powerful suction, this robot vacuum efficiently picks up dirt, dust, pet hair, and crumbs from deep within carpets, tiles, and hard-to-reach spaces, ensuring a cleaner home every time.

The Q10 S5+ features a jaw scrapers comb and a specialized side brush to prevent hair from getting tangled, thereby improving cleaning efficiency and preventing clogs.
